## üìù Tips for Recording:

1. **Screen Resolution**: Use 1920x1080 or 1280x720
2. **Font Size**: Increase terminal and editor font for visibility
3. **Recording Software**: OBS Studio, ShareX, or built-in Windows Game Bar
4. **Audio**: Clear microphone, minimal background noise
5. **Speed**: Speak slowly and clearly
6. **Cursor**: Make cursor movements deliberate and visible
7. **Pauses**: Pause briefly to let viewers read outputs
